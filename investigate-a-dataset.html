Introduction¶

In this project i am going to be analyzing data of patient appointment, the main goal is to predict if patient will show up for their scheduled appointment or not and to determine how patients who show up for their appointment differs from patients who do not show up for their appointment. The dataset collects information from 100 thousands medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on whether or not patients show up for their appointment. A number of characteristics about the patient are included in each row.Some of the vital columns i have to pay attention to when analyzing the data are, ScheduledDay which provides data on what day the patient set up their appointment.

Neighborhood which provides data on the location of the hospital.

Scholarship which provides data on whether or not the patient is enrolled in Brasilian welfare program Bolsa Famlia.

No-show provides data of wether patient will show up for the appointment or not

Neighborhood provides data of where the patient live.

My Independent variables here are ScheduledDay, Scholarship and Handicap. And my dependent variable is No-show

# Define a function to plot horizontal barcharts
def horizontalbarChart(Dataframe, x_label, y_label, title, size):
    Dataframe.plot.barh(figsize=size)
    plt.xlabel(x_label)
    plt.ylabel(y_label)
    plt.title(title, fontsize=14)
    plt.show()
# Define a function to plot Vertical barcharts
def verticalbarChart(Dataframe, x_label, y_label, title, size):
    Dataframe.plot.bar(figsize=size)
    plt.xlabel(x_label)
    plt.ylabel(y_label)
    plt.title(title, fontsize=14)
    plt.show()

Conclusions¶

From the above analysis, i found out that NoShow is affected by the scheduleday, whether the patient is handicap or not, that is patients with crucial handicap situations would likely not show up for their appointments, Scholarship and SMS_received are also independent variables which NoShow depends on. The effect of Hypertension and gender to NoShow colum is very minimal.

To conclude, for a health center to improve in the number of patients who respect their appointappointments, they need to take into consideration ScheduledDay, Scholarship and if the patient is HAndicap. Also they have to consider sending reminder SMS to their patients concerning their scheduled appointments. from the above analysis, i can't really conclude on the effect of gender on noshow because the number of females are significantly greater than the number of males, again Health centers can consider providing means for Handicap patients so that they can start showing up for their appointments or they can consider remote appointments.Appointment date has a sif
